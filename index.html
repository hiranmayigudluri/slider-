Deadpool & Wolverine is a 2024 American superhero film based on Marvel Comics featuring the characters Deadpool and Wolverine. Produced by Marvel Studios, and 21 Laps Entertainment, it is the 34th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)
